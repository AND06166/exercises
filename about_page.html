About

World Plant Exchange is a place where people from all around the world can share plants and seeds of all types. If you can't find the plant or seed you're looking for then you may make a request and somebody may have what you're looking for. You may offer plants or seeds to share with others whether or not you want anything in return.

World Plant Exchange allows you to submit offers and requests for plants and seeds to be viewed by other people from around the world. You may use the simple form to add an offer or request quickly, or the advanced form to customize your submission, making it easier and more accurate for other people to search for what they're looking for.

Communicate easily with other users using the private messaging system.

The Sent and Received offers screens make it easy to keep track of offers to and sent by you.

Users may track and update the status of their trades with the 'My Trades' feature.

Users may be rated by the people they trade with so others will know whether or not the person they're trading with is a reliable trader.

You may add your submissions to your profile so that other users may see your offers and requests more easily.

The advanced search functionality allows you to search for seeds, bulbs, cuttings, and plants by a variety of characteristics. Submissions may be searched for by common name, botanical name, color, type of plant, location, and growing difficulty. So, whether you would like to grow an all blue flower garden, grow plants from South America or start a medicinal herb or vegetable garden, the World Plant Exchange community can help.

World Plant Exchange is free to use for everyone!
